#BlackLivesMatter

I am donating 50% of my royalties to The Bail Project

Police Brutality is not OK. Systemic Racism is not OK. These issues are inexorably linked and this, too, is not OK. The United States has a long history with both systemic racism as well as police brutality. This is not OK.

I cannot put my body on the line, nor can I address the problem through voting (I live in Washington, DC and have no representation in Congress).

As such, I will be donating 50% of the royalties I make from sales of my books from now until the end of 2020 to The Bail Project.

How Will This Work?

I will post my revenue, royalties, and donations here periodically. At least monthly. If you would like to donate to The Bail Project, but don't need technical non-fiction, please follow the link and donate directly.
